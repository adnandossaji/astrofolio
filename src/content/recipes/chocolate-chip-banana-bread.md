# Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

This banana bread recipe is the perfect way to use up overripe bananas! The addition of chocolate chips makes it extra special, and the sour cream ensures it stays moist for days. Enjoy a slice for breakfast with your morning coffee or warm it up with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for dessert.
